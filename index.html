What we are doing
Audio augmentation is a set of techniques used to enhance and transform audio data to create variations that help improve the performance and robustness of machine learning models, especially in tasks related to speech recognition, music genre classification, and other audio-based applications. This technique helps in creating a more diverse dataset, which leads to better generalization and performance of audio-based machine learning models.
Why audio
Go onto the web and search: There are thousands of sites and tools that help developers augment their images, text and other kinds of data, but when u switch to audio, there are only a few tools that is very much intended for experienced programmers building their own project, however, what about musicians who have little experience in these fields? what abput music lovers who would like to create an AI of their own with a specific style? What about those who are less experienced? Thats who our site is serving, as we want to provide a more accessabile tool for music and deep learning.
